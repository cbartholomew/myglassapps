Synchronize The Service

  1. Select the blog that you wish to share
  2. Choose the Sync w/ Glass Button
  3. Head over to https://glass.google.com/myglass/share and "toggle-on" the "Blogger" contact.
  4. You can unbind your blog selection by choosing the "Desynchronize Service" below.

Using the Service

Take a picture using Glass and share it with your "Blogger", shared contact. Afterwards, a Timeline card will inserted once your post has been sent to your Blog. The Timeline card also comes with these avaliable options.

Icon Description Action
Reply Using Reply will allow you to change the post's default title (#throughglass) using the Glass Device's voice UI.
Delete Will remove the item from the Timeline only - will NOT remove post from blogger.
Remove Post Will remove the item from the Timeline and WILL remove post from blogger.
